During night, the streets are lit up with street lights. Find the length of each street that receives street light.
Given:
-Specifications of the street.
-There are total L street lights.
- Each light I cover the street from Xi to Yi along the street. 
Input:
Number of light
Array[][] L*2, (xi,yi) will show that light covers from xi to yi.
Sample:
Input:
1
5 10
Output:
5
Explanation:
Light 1: 10-5 = 5 unit covered.
